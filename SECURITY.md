# Reporting Vulnerabilities

Our team, like everyone else, is human; we make mistakes. In the case that we do, we need people like you to point them out to us so that we can fix them.

To get in contact with us, either:
- Email [security@whisp.bot](mailto:security@whisp.bot?subject=%5BUrgent%5D%20Vulnerability%20Report&body=Description%3A%0D%0A*A%20clear%20and%20concise%20description%20of%20the%20vulnerability.*%0D%0A%0D%0AReproduction%20Steps%3A%0D%0A1.%20Steps%20on%0D%0A2.%20How%20to%0D%0A3.%20Reproduce%0D%0A4.%20The%20Vulnerability%0D%0A%0D%0AExpected%20Behaviour%3A%0D%0A*What%20you%20expected%20to%20happen.*%0D%0A%0D%0AAdditional%20Context%3A%0D%0A*Other%20context%20such%20as%20platform%2C%20external%20tools%2C%20etc.*%0D%0A%0D%0A*Attach%20screenshots%20if%20applicable.*).
- Open a ticket in our [Discord](https://whisp.bot/support).
and provide as much information as possible that will help us pin-point the issue.

Expect a response within 48 hours with details about the status of your report and what we will be doing to make sure that the vulerability is fixed.
